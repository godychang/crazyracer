Game Overview

Crazy Racer is an exciting multiplayer racing game where you can challenge your friends in intense head-to-head competitions. With stunning graphics and responsive controls, this game offers an immersive racing experience right in your browser.

Race through various challenging tracks, unlock new vehicles, and prove you're the ultimate racer!

How to Play

Player 1 Controls:

  • W - Accelerate
  • S - Brake/Reverse
  • A - Turn Left
  • D - Turn Right

Player 2 Controls:

  • Arrow Up - Accelerate
  • Arrow Down - Brake/Reverse
  • Arrow Left - Turn Left
  • Arrow Right - Turn Right
